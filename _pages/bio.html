Plug n Play

University of Baguio is a home of talented and versatile students. Plug and play is a part of the performing arts community in the University, which is composed of students, who have a desire and love for music, coming from different schools. They show the variety because together with their vocalist, they also play different instruments such as the guitar, bass, piano, and drums. The band plays different genres in harmony that suits that taste of their crowd. Every semester, the band recruits new members to give equal opportunities to join them. As they say, without music life would be boring. So what are you waiting for? Audition now and be one of them.

Enjoy incredible experiences while playing on stage and perks of joining exclusive events of the University.

If you have the desire and guts to be part of the band, come and join them now. For more information, just proceed to the music studio and find sir Kherl Victoria.
